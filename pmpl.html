PMPL facebook group app for administrators

Administrators of the facebook group, Pacenet Meghbela Broadband (PMPL) Forum, have built the Pacenet Meghbela Broadband Forum app to manage the group more effectively.

Users can't login or use this app. This app posts on behalf of the page 'Meghbela Broadband FB Group Admin' which is linked with the group. Page token is inserted manually by the admins

The scope of this app is describe below

  • Search for certain keyworks in the group feed and post automatic pre-composed replies if a match is found
  • Comment on old posts which have no replies and bring them on top
  • Post automatic admin announcements
